Grassroots Hospitality.

GrassRoots Hospitality is a hosting project based on the values of the GrassRoots Movement and is focused on helping the Alternative and Solidarity Community to grow in a sustainable way and to unlock value of space while combatting gentrification and extraction of local value. On one hand is a commons of fair priced spaces for visitors to have comfortable, value-aligned space to stay; on the other hand is a project that supports activists coming from abroad or other parts of the country by actively nurturing relationships with a city wide network of Hosts and Local Communities and Projects. After our experience in different groups and projects we understand the need of hosting actvists when they travel to Athens in order to attend festivals, conferences, workshops, work in local projects, or when they visit for solidarity reasons for demonstrations etc.. We also aim to facilitate integrating activists in the local community while they are here.
